Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a custom gate cost?

Single walkway gates typically start around $1,200 and RV gates around $2,400, depending on size, materials, and hardware. Every quote is firm — the number we give you is the number you pay.

How long does it take?

Most gates are fabricated and installed within 1–2 weeks of your deposit. Trellises and smaller pieces are often faster.

What materials do you use?

Heavy-gauge steel frames with powder-coat finish, and your choice of infill — composite wood-look slats (no rot, no warping), wrought iron pickets, or perforated metal for privacy.

Do you do repairs on existing gates?

Yes — sagging gates, broken hinges, rusted latches, and re-welds. If it's metal, we can usually fix it.

What areas do you serve?

The greater Phoenix metro — Mesa, Gilbert, Chandler, Tempe, Scottsdale, Queen Creek, and surrounding areas.
